About Me

My name is Bennett Van Soelen. I was born in Indianapolis, Indiana on February 25th, 2000. I grew up on the north side of Indy in the City of Carmel. Fun fact: Carmel, Indiana has the most roundabouts out of every city in the world. We currently have 125 roundabouts and you typically don't have to stop whenever you drive through the city.

Some of my favorite things to do include playing basketball, spending time with friends, playing videogames, and going to concerts.
