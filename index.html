About Me

My name is Bennett Van Soelen. I was born in Indianapolis, Indiana on February 25th, 2000. I grew up on the north side of Indy in the City of Carmel. Fun fact: Carmel, Indiana has the most roundabouts out of every city in the world. We currently have 125 roundabouts and you typically don't have to stop whenever you drive through the city.

What I like

Some of my favorite things to do include playing basketball, spending time with friends, playing videogames, and going to concerts. Unfortunately, due to covid, I haven't been able to go to a concert since February of 2020. While this is a first world problem I still would like to be in a large crowd again. Despite not being able to go to concerts, I still have been keeping up with the things I enjoy. Last semester, our group was involved in a friendly four team basketball league that played twice a week. This gave everyone the opportunity to get outside and active after sitting on zoom calls all day. The league definitely helped improve morale among our friends. I also have spent a lot of time playing videogames. One of my favorite games is Stardew Valley. In the Valley you can farm, fish, mine, make friends, and much more. I have no limit to the amount of time that I spend on that game. When I'm not doing the things that I enjoy, I'm probably completing schoolwork or looking for a job.
